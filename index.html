About Alpha Turf

Alpha Turf is Lucknow's newest premium sports facility, designed for athletes and sports enthusiasts who demand excellence. Our state-of-the-art artificial turf provides the perfect playing surface for both football and cricket.

We offer flexible hourly booking slots to accommodate your schedule, whether you're training with your team, organizing a friendly match, or honing your individual skills. Experience the difference that quality infrastructure makes in your game.
